Chocolate Salami

Description

Contrary to the name, there is actually no salami in this recipe. Sorry to disappoint. Instead, enjoy this sweet cookie!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sun dried cherries
  • 8 oz semisweet chocolate
  • 4 tsps butter
  • 8 tsps sugar
  • 2 oz finely chopped almonds
  • 2 oz finely chopped pistachios
  • 6 oz crushed biscotti
  • 2 tsps candied orange peel
  • 1 oz Grand Marnier
  • 3 egg yolks

Steps

  1. Soak cherries in warm water for about 30 minutes and drain well
  2. Place chocolate in bowl with butter over a pot of simmering water until melted
  3. Add sugar, almonds, pistachios, biscotti, drained cherries, orange peel, Grand Marnier and mix well
  4. Remove from heat, add egg yolks and mix well
  5. Cool completely before forming a salami shape by rolling mixture in a sheet of wax paper
  6. Refrigerate 4 hours or overnight
  7. Slice, serve and enjoy!
